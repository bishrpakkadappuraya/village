Welcome to Pakkadappuraya

A Hidden Gem in Kerala

Nestled amidst the lush green landscapes of Kerala, Pakkadappuraya is a picturesque village that offers visitors a glimpse into the authentic rural life of God's Own Country. With its pristine paddy fields, coconut groves, and traditional architecture, our village stands as a testament to Kerala's rich cultural heritage.

The name "Pakkadappuraya" derives from the Malayalam words meaning "neighboring kitchen," reflecting our community's warm hospitality and the importance of shared meals in our culture. For generations, our village has preserved traditional farming methods, art forms, and culinary practices that visitors can experience firsthand.

Village Attractions

Discover the unique experiences that make Pakkadappuraya a must-visit destination for travelers seeking authentic cultural immersion.

Traditional Farming Experience

Join local farmers in their daily activities, from paddy planting to harvesting. Learn ancient agricultural techniques that have been passed down through generations.

Culinary Experiences

Savor authentic Kerala cuisine prepared using traditional methods. Participate in cooking classes to learn the secrets behind our flavorful dishes and spice blends.

Cultural Performances

Witness captivating performances of traditional art forms like Kathakali, Mohiniyattam, and Theyyam. Experience the rich cultural heritage of Kerala through music and dance.
